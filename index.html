Welcome to my website!

I retired at the end of Spring semester 2024, but you can still find me at my appstate email address (hirsthp at appstate.edu) I am working on a repository of modeling projects and a set of materials for computational mathematics. I am the editor of The Centroid, the Journal of the North Carolina Council of Teachers of Mathmathics, and the techical editor for the Journal of Computational Science Education. Prior to retiring, I served as the math MA program director or co-director (1995-05, 2013-20, 2022-23), as Acting Assistant Dean in the College of Arts and Sciences (1998-99), as SACS Self-study Director (1999-02), and as Associate Dean for Research and Graduate Studies (2005-13).
