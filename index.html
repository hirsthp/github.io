Service

  • assessment coordinator for the BA and BS in Mathematics
  • webmaster for the Department of Mathematical Sciences website.
  • chair of Appalachian's Graduate Academic Policies and Procedures (GAPP) Committee
  • chair of Appalachian's Scholarship Advisory Council
  • member of Appalachian's Arts and Cultural Programs Advisory Committee
  • thesis and dissertation reader for Appalachian's School of Graduate Studies
  • editor for The Centroid, the Journal of the North Carolina Council of Teachers of Mathematics
  • mathematics editor for the Journal of Computational Science Education (JOCSE)
  • member of the Board of Directors for Shodor

I have served on the Mathematical Association of America's Committee on the Mathematical Education of Teachers (COMET) from 2009 through 2014, and on the Education Committees and as coordinator for the Mathematics Workshop sessions for Super Computing 2011 (SC11) and Super Computing 2010 (SC10). I also served as regional coordinator for the Mathematical Association of America's Preparing Mathematicians to Educate Teachers (PMET) initiative and as co-PI on the NSF funded National Computational Science Institute and Shodor Computational Science Institute. I also help when I can with Project Interactivate and other mathematics and computational science initiatives at Shodor .

On campus, I have served as the math MA program director or co-director (1995-05, 2013-20, 2022), as Acting Assistant Dean in the College of Arts and Sciences (1998-99), as SACS Self-study Director (1999-02), and as Associate Dean in the Cratis Williams Graduate School (2005-13).
